I

INTERACTION PHASE
  1. Register and First interaction meeting
  2. Measurement of site
  3. Understanding the requirements and client's brief
  4. Providing initial layouts / concepts ideas
  5. Finalizing the scope and commercials of project

II

DESIGN PHASE
  1. Layout Finalization
  2. Understanding the client's needs via initial presentations
  3. 3D visual render presentations to show actual design output
  4. Finalization of designs after thorough discussions
  5. Issuing relevant working drawings on site

III

EXECUTION PHASE
  1. Time-to-time Supervision of site work w.r.t drawings issued
  2. Assisting clients with contractors
  3. Finalization of Materials/Colors schemes
  4. Assisting clients with Furniture and furnishings
  5. Maintaining work quality check and timely completion of work
